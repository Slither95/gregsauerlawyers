Greg Sauer, Attorney at Law

Greg Sauer

Greg Sauer earned his Bachelor of Arts in Political Science at The University of Dayton in 1983. He went on to The University of Cincinnati College of Law graduating in 1986. Greg began in private practice of law in November of 1986. He started his primary focus on Personal Injury and Work Related Cases. Over his 32 years of practice he has become experienced in a variety of legal areas including Real Estate, Criminal Litigation, Corporate Small Business Law, Family Law, Probate and Estate Planning.

He not only knows the law, he continues to educate himself in new laws that are consistently changing. He understands when you hire an attorney it is because you are going through an area in your life you don't specialize in. Greg will take the time to educate you on the law regarding your legal issue giving you a list of possible options and courses of action.

He understands how important your legal issue is to you and he fights on your behalf. Greg is ethical, competent, and believes in an open line of communication. He strives to save you trouble, time, and money on all your legal issues.
