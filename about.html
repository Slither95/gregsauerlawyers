Greg Sauer - Estate Planning Attorney Since 1986

Greg Sauer

For nearly 40 years, Greg Sauer has helped Ohio individuals and families navigate important legal matters with confidence and peace of mind.

Throughout his career, Greg has represented clients in a variety of legal areas, providing practical guidance and trusted counsel to individuals, families, and business owners. Today, he focuses his practice on estate planning, helping clients protect their loved ones, preserve their assets, and prepare for the future.

Greg's estate planning services include:

  • Wills
  • Revocable Living Trusts
  • Living Wills
  • Healthcare Powers of Attorney
  • Durable Financial Powers of Attorney
  • Probate Avoidance Planning
  • Estate Plan Reviews and Updates

Greg believes estate planning should be straightforward and understandable. He takes the time to explain your options, answer your questions, and create a plan tailored to your family's unique needs and goals.

Whether you are creating your first estate plan, updating existing documents, or planning for future generations, Greg is committed to providing personal attention and trusted legal guidance every step of the way.
